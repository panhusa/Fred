My story

Hi there, my name is Fredy Bony, I am an Ivorian born singer-songwriter with over 25 songs released independently. I performed in over three continents and in 3 languages including mandarin. I am a multi instrumentalist artist who excels in the art of story telling.

Starting out as a rapper, from Ivory Coast to Czechia passing by Taiwan, I now speak fluently French, English and Mandarin and I have slowly shifted my style from rap to a more folk and pop sound.

With my ability to paint pictures, tell stories and uplift others using my music, I never failed to provide a positive and soothing experience to my listeners.

Also, I am a true student of the independent artists’ school. Inspired by artists like Russ, Nipsey hustle and Ryan Leslie, I have been at the steer of every aspect of my career. I am a creative businessman.

Being a lifelong learner, I’m planning to push the boundaries of what is expected of me as an artist or a black person. I strongly believe that, freedom of one self lies in his/her willingness to learn something new everyday.

I intend to keep growing as an artist, motivate and inspire others to find themselves and break free from the box that the world is trying to put them in. As one of my favourite artists Kanye west said « Birds can’t fly in a cage».

Freedom to be ourselves. That is my number one goal.
